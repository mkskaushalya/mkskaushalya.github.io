About me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm a highly experienced full-stack developer with a passion for design and a solid technical background. With expertise in JavaScript, Node, Python, Java, C++, React, Next JS, Laravel, PHP with OOP, and more, I specialize in creating stunning, user-friendly websites and applications.

Some of my notable projects include:

  • LETS talk 2022 Official Website: An interactive platform built for IEEE YPSL.
  • Real-Time Web-Based Chat Application: A dynamic chat application developed using modern web technologies.
  • SLTC Research University: A comprehensive web solution for the university community.
  • Month of CodegeniX 2022 official websites: A series of websites showcasing coding challenges and events.
  • IEEE Education Week Sri Lanka 2024 Website: A platform promoting educational events organized by IEEE Sri Lanka Section.

In addition to my projects, I've held key roles in various organizations, such as Webmaster for IEEE Student Branch of SLTC and Technical Team Lead at CodeMania v3.0. I'm also proficient in UI/UX Design, AWS, Git, Software Design Patterns, Data Structures, and Algorithms.

My educational background includes a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from SLTC and a Diploma in Software Engineering from SLIIT.

I hold certifications in Full-stack Web Development, UI/UX Design, AWS Academy Cloud Foundations, and have secured second place in CodeMania v2.0 competition.

Beyond my technical skills, I'm deeply involved in volunteering, blogging, and content creation. My soft skills include problem-solving, time management, creativity, and critical thinking.

Why You Should Join The Event

Booths

Exhibitors often represent educational institutions, organizations, and companies that are leaders in the field of education. Visiting these booths can provide attendees with a chance to learn about new products and services.

Experience

Provides an opportunity for individuals to learn about the latest trends, research, and innovations in the field of education, connect with like-minded individuals, and improve their skills and knowledge.

Networking

Allows attendees to participate in presentations, workshops, and panel discussions where they can meet new people, share ideas, and discuss issues related to education that can lead to future opportunities.

Resources

Offers a range of resources and opportunities to help individuals develop their skills and knowledge.

Cutting-edge Technologies

Presents a unique opportunity to learn about current and emerging technologies through a plethora of technical sessions, group activities, hands-on workshops, panel discussions, etc.

Proceed Forward

The economic crisis of the country has had a major impact on engineers and technologists, and now is the time to find the training and educational opportunities they need.
